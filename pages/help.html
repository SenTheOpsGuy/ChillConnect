Help Center

Find answers to your questions and get the support you need.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?
Click on "Get Started" and fill out the registration form. You'll need to verify your email address and complete your profile to start using ChillConnect.
What types of users can join ChillConnect?
ChillConnect welcomes both service seekers and service providers. Seekers can browse and book services, while providers can offer their services to our community.
How does verification work?
All users go through our comprehensive verification process including email verification, phone verification, and document verification for providers. This ensures a safe and trusted community.

Booking & Payments

How do I make a booking?
Browse providers, read their profiles and reviews, then click "Book Now" to initiate a booking. You can message providers before booking to discuss details.
What payment methods are accepted?
We use a secure token-based payment system. You can purchase tokens using major credit cards, debit cards, or digital wallets. All transactions are encrypted and secure.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled case-by-case based on our refund policy. Contact our support team if you need to cancel a booking or request a refund.

Safety & Security

How do you ensure user safety?
We implement multiple safety measures including background verification, 24/7 moderation, encrypted communications, secure payments with escrow protection, and an emergency reporting system.
How do I report inappropriate behavior?
Use the report button in chat or on user profiles. You can also contact our support team directly. All reports are taken seriously and investigated promptly.
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. We never share personal information without your consent. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
