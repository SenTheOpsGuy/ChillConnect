Meeting Safely

üè¢

Public Places

Always meet in busy, public locations with good lighting and security. Coffee shops, restaurants, and shopping centers are ideal choices.

üöó

Your Transportation

Use your own transportation or trusted ride services. Don't accept rides from people you've just met or let them know your home address initially.

üì±

Stay Connected

Keep your phone charged and accessible. Consider sharing your live location with a trusted contact during the meeting.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong or uncomfortable, trust your gut feeling. It's always okay to leave a situation that doesn't feel right.

Red Flags to Watch For

  • Pressure to meet immediately or in private locations
  • Inconsistent information or stories
  • Reluctance to communicate on the platform
  • Requests for personal information too quickly
  • No profile photos or verification

Financial Safety

üí≥

Use Platform Payment System

Always use ChillConnect's secure token system for payments. Never share your bank details, credit card information, or make direct transfers.

Avoid Cash Transactions

Our escrow system protects both parties. Avoid cash payments as they don't offer the same protection and dispute resolution options.

üö´

Never Send Money Outside the Platform

Scammers often ask for payments outside our secure system. Never send money via wire transfer, gift cards, or other payment methods outside ChillConnect.

Online Safety

üîê

Protect Your Personal Information

Don't share your full name, home address, workplace, or financial information until you've established trust through multiple interactions.

üì∏

Be Careful with Photos

Avoid sharing photos that reveal personal information like your home address, license plates, or workplace. Use recent, appropriate photos on your profile.

üîí

Secure Your Account

Use a strong, unique password for your ChillConnect account. Enable two-factor authentication if available and log out from shared devices.
