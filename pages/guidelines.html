Our Promise: ChillConnect is committed to creating a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment where all users can connect and engage positively. These guidelines help ensure our community remains welcoming for everyone.

  • Use polite and professional language

  • Harass, bully, or intimidate other users
  • Make threats or engage in hostile behavior
  • Share hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Target users based on their identity or background

2. Safety and Security

Safety First: Your safety is our top priority. Follow these guidelines to protect yourself and others in our community.

2.1 Personal Safety

  • Meet in safe, public locations for in-person services
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, report it
  • Don't share personal financial information
  • Use ChillConnect's communication tools until you're comfortable
  • Inform a friend or family member about your plans

2.2 Platform Security

  • Keep your login credentials secure
  • Report suspicious accounts or behavior immediately
  • Don't share account access with others
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Log out from shared devices

3. Honest and Accurate Information

‚úÖ Authentic Profiles

  • Use real, recent photos of yourself
  • Provide accurate service descriptions
  • Be honest about your skills and experience
  • Update your availability regularly
  • Complete verification processes truthfully

‚ùå Misleading Content

  • Use fake photos or impersonate others
  • Provide false or misleading information
  • Exaggerate qualifications or experience
  • Create multiple accounts for the same person
  • Manipulate reviews or ratings

4. Appropriate Content

4.1 Profile Content

  • Use appropriate, professional photos
  • Write clear, respectful service descriptions
  • Keep content relevant to your services
  • Follow local laws and regulations

4.2 Communication Standards

  • Keep conversations relevant to services
  • Maintain professional boundaries
  • Respect others' time and availability
  • Use appropriate language at all times

Prohibited Content: We strictly prohibit illegal content, explicit material, spam, scams, or any content that violates local laws or our terms of service.

5. Fair Business Practices

5.1 Service Providers

  • Deliver services as described in your profile
  • Communicate clearly about pricing and expectations
  • Be punctual and professional
  • Honor confirmed bookings
  • Provide excellent customer service

5.2 Service Seekers

  • Be clear about your requirements and expectations
  • Respect agreed-upon terms and pricing
  • Provide honest feedback and reviews
  • Communicate promptly about changes or cancellations
  • Pay for services as agreed

6. Intellectual Property and Privacy

  • Respect others' intellectual property rights
  • Don't share copyrighted content without permission
  • Protect other users' personal information
  • Don't screenshot or share private conversations
  • Respect photography and content usage rights

7. Platform Usage

7.1 Proper Use

  • Use ChillConnect only for its intended purpose
  • Follow all technical guidelines and limitations
  • Don't attempt to circumvent platform features
  • Respect other users' profiles and boundaries

7.2 Prohibited Activities

  • Spam or unsolicited messaging
  • Automated account creation or management
  • Attempting to hack or exploit the platform
  • Using the platform for illegal activities
  • Circumventing payment systems

8. Consequences and Enforcement

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

  • Warning: First-time minor violations
  • Content Removal: Inappropriate posts or profile content
  • Feature Restrictions: Limited access to certain platform features
  • Account Suspension: Temporary removal from the platform
  • Permanent Ban: Permanent removal for serious or repeated violations

Zero Tolerance: We have zero tolerance for illegal activities, harassment, discrimination, or behavior that threatens user safety. Such violations may result in immediate account termination and reporting to authorities.

10. Updates to Guidelines

These Community Guidelines may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our community standards or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes through:

  • Email notifications
  • In-app announcements
  • Platform notifications

Continued use of ChillConnect constitutes acceptance of updated guidelines.

Thank You: By following these guidelines, you help create a positive environment where everyone can connect safely and successfully. Together, we build a stronger, more inclusive community.
