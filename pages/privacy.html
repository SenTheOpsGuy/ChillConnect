Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: This Privacy Policy explains how ChillConnect collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to maintaining your trust and protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Profile information and photos
  • Identity verification documents
  • Payment information (securely processed)
  • Communication preferences

1.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect information about how you use our platform:

  • Device information (type, operating system, browser)
  • IP address and location data
  • Platform usage patterns and preferences
  • Interaction data (messages, bookings, reviews)

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from third-party services used for verification, payment processing, or platform enhancement.

2. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Information Used
Account Creation & Management Personal information, verification documents
Platform Services Profile data, preferences, usage information
Safety & Security All collected information for verification and moderation
Communications Contact information, communication preferences
Platform Improvement Usage patterns, feedback, analytics data

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 When We Share Information

We only share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Limited profile information is visible to facilitate connections
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist with platform operations
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers or acquisitions

3.2 What We Don't Share

We never sell your personal information to third parties or use it for purposes other than those described in this policy.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with access controls
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security assessments
  • Employee Training: Staff are trained on data protection practices
  • Incident Response: We have procedures for handling security incidents

5. Your Privacy Rights

Access Your Data

Request a copy of the personal information we have about you.

Update Information

Correct or update your personal information at any time.

Delete Your Account

Request deletion of your account and associated data.

Data Portability

Request your data in a portable format to transfer elsewhere.

Opt-Out

Unsubscribe from marketing communications or certain data uses.

Object to Processing

Object to certain types of data processing in specific circumstances.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Provide personalized experiences
  • Ensure platform security

You can control cookie settings through your browser. See our Cookie Policy for detailed information.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Improve our platform and services

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. International Data Transfers

ChillConnect primarily operates in India. If you access our platform from other countries, your information may be transferred to and processed in India. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

9. Children's Privacy

ChillConnect is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email notification to your registered email address
  • Prominent notice on our platform
  • In-app notification

Your continued use of ChillConnect after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
